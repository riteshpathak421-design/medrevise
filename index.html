⏰ Set up Siri reminder
2 minutes • built into your iPhone • works offline
How it works: iPhone's Shortcuts app lets you create a daily automation that fires at any time — with full sound, on lock screen, even in airplane mode. No extra apps needed!
1
Open the Shortcuts app
It's already on your iPhone. Search for "Shortcuts" in Spotlight if you can't find it.
2
Tap "Automation" tab (bottom)
Then tap "New Automation" or the + button at top right.
3
Choose "Time of Day"
Set your preferred time (e.g. 8:00 AM). Set Repeat → Daily. Tap Next.
4
Add action: "Show Notification"
Tap "Add Action" → search "notification" → select "Show Notification".
Set the text to: Time to revise! Open MedRevise 🌸
Notification text: "Time to revise! Open MedRevise 🌸"
5
Turn OFF "Ask Before Running"
Tap Next → toggle "Ask Before Running" to OFF → tap Done.
6
Done! 🎉
Every day at your set time, your iPhone will show a notification with sound. Tap it → open MedRevise → see what's due!
