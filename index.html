Set iPhone alarm as your reminder
Since web apps cannot ring your iPhone in the background, use the built-in Clock app as your alarm. When it rings — open MedRevise and the app will chime and show exactly what to revise.
How to set up your study alarm:
  • Open iPhone Clock app → tap Alarm tab at the bottom
  • Tap the + button (top right) to add a new alarm
  • Set your preferred study time (e.g. 7:00 AM)
  • Tap Label → tap Copy below → paste as the label
  • Tap Repeat → select Every Day
  • Choose a loud ringtone → tap Save
